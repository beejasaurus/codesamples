Grand Master of U.S. Royal Martial Arts


Grand Master Jae Sic Choi


Master Choi is a 5th degree Tae Kwon Do Black Belt. He has been teaching martial arts to students of all ages, with skill levels ranging from beginners to professional demonstration and sparring teams for nearly 20 years.

Master Choi is also one of few 6th degree Hap Ki Do Black Belts in the USA. U.S Royal Martial Arts is designated as the American Headquarters of the World Hap Ki Do Federation.
