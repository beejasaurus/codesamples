U.S. Royal Martial Arts offers training in both Tae Kwon Do and Hap Ki Do for children, teens, and adults. The school is associated with World Tae Kwon Do and the Hap Ki Do Federation. The school is located in the Loehmann’s Plaza.

Owner and Head Instructor Master Jae Sic Choi is a National Hap Ki Do Competition Form Champion, recipient of the National Hap Ki Do Special Award, and a bronze medalist in the South Korean Military Tae Kwon Do Competition.
